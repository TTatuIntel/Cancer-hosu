Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for membership? +

Complete the online application form, provide required documentation including your medical license and CV, and submit the application fee. Our membership committee reviews applications monthly.

What are the membership requirements? +

Applicants must be licensed healthcare professionals working in oncology or related fields. Additional requirements vary by membership tier and are detailed in our membership guidelines.

Can I upgrade my membership? +

Yes, members can upgrade their membership tier at any time. The difference in fees will be prorated for the remainder of your membership year.
