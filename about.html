Our Purpose

Our Mission

To cure or control cancer through Research, Education, and Service delivery. We are committed to improving cancer outcomes for all Ugandans.

Our Vision

No one should die of cancer. We envision a Uganda where every person has access to quality cancer prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and care.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence — Highest standards in clinical care & research
  • Compassion — Dignity, empathy & respect for every patient
  • Collaboration — Partnerships across institutions & borders
  • Integrity — Ethical practice & transparency
  • Innovation — Evidence-based approaches & new technologies
