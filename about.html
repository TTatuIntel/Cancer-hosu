About HOSU

The Hematology & Oncology Society of Uganda (HOSU) is fully registered as a company limited by guarantee by the Uganda Registration Services Bureau. Registration No: 80034950959778

Our Mission

To cure or control cancer through Research, Education, and Service delivery.

Our Vision

No one should die of cancer.

Our Objectives

🔬

Promote Evidence-Based Practice

Advance scientific knowledge and evidence-based practice in Haematology and Oncology care in Uganda.

🤝

Improve Accessibility

Implement strategies to enhance quality and accessibility of Haematology and Oncology services.

💡

Education and Training

Promote comprehensive education and training for all healthcare cadres in Haematology and Oncology.

❤️

Research Excellence

Support cutting-edge research in Haematology and Oncology specific to Uganda's needs.

🔍

Quality Assurance

Ensure quality assurance in training, research, and service delivery.

👥

Member Advocacy

Represent, promote, and defend the rights of HOSU members.

📄

Knowledge Dissemination

Synthesize, disseminate, and evaluate information relevant to Haematology and Oncology practice in Uganda.

💰

Member Support

Provide financial support, resources, and services to improve members' quality of life.
